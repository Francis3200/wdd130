Santo Domingo Norte is my favorite city because it combines the tranquility of residential neighborhoods with the vibrant energy of a constantly growing area. Places like Villa Mella and Sabana Perdida reflect the daily life of its hardworking people, their sense of community, and their culture. It also has access to the Santo Domingo Metro, which makes transportation and connections to other parts of the capital easy. It's a city full of life, history, and opportunities, where you can truly feel the spirit of Greater Santo Domingo.
